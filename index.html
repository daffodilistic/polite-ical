POLITE STMS Calendar Generator

Load an .xlsx file from POLITEMall and export a calendar-ready .ics file.

How to Use

  1. Access STMS and look for your course (in the "My Course" section).
  2. Under "Course details", click on the Semester/Term tab to view your modules.
  3. After selecting the module you want to export, click on the "Download" button in the "Timetabling & attendance information" section to download the .xlsx file.
  4. Use the file input below to load the downloaded .xlsx file.
